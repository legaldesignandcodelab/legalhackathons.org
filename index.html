// about

Why a legal hackathon?

AI is transforming every industry. But the legal sector needs more than off-the-shelf tools — it needs precision, ethics, and domain expertise.

Problem

Innovation gap

Legal work demands more than just speed; it requires precision, domain expertise, and accountability to handle complex nuances. Specialized solutions co-developed between law and tech folks are thus needed.

Solution

24h sandbox

Legal expertise meets engineering in a risk-free environment to prototype real tools using AI and LLMs.

Impact

Access to justice

Practical, ethical, and secure tools that increase efficiency and broaden access to justice for the public.

// challenges

Challenge tracks

Each sponsor presents a real-world legal-AI problem. Choose the one that excites you most. Final challenge descriptions will be announced closer to the event, they will cover these themes.

Contract analysis

AI-powered clause detection, risk scoring, and automated review.

Legal research

Smarter case law search, precedent matching, and knowledge graphs.

Access to justice

Tools that make legal services accessible to non-experts.

Legal design

Human-centered interfaces for legal documents and processes.

Efficient processes

Optimizing legal and justice operations — reducing delays, cutting costs, improving access to courts and legal services.

Regulatory intelligence

Monitoring, analyzing, and implementing new laws and regulations at speed — helping firms stay compliant and competitive.
